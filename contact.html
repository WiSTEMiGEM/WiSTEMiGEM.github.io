Make a submission to the Equality Pursuit magazine

We are looking for various forms of creative expression to highlight the importance of gender inclusivity in STEM through amplifying the voices of women and underrepresented gender communities. Visual art, photographs, poetry, short comics, and pieces of writing accepted.

Email us for more information about the length and type of your submission as well as any questions. Thank you for your interest, and we look forward to receiving your work!
